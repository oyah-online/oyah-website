For ordering and earning the points.

  • Check in to display the menu, view friends and other favorites.
  • Order and pay.
  • You will be notified once order is ready to pick it up.
  • Review and share your favorite orders to earn the sharing points.

For sending and redeeming gifts.

To send a gift.

  • Click on the gift icon and select the user you want to gift.
  • select one of the available stores and select the items you want to gift.
  • Write something and pay.
  • Gift will be sent.

To redeem the gift.

  • Check in at store to display the menu.
  • You will find the gift in the menu.
  • Add to the cart and check out.
  • You will be notified once it is ready to pick it up.

To earn and rewards your friends with extra points.

  • Check in and order from someone favorite menu.
  • You will earn the ordering points and he/she will earn the referring points.
